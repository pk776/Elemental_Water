About Elemental

We make playful, sustainable water bottles for people who like to stand out.

Who we are

Elemental blends creativity and humor to build bottles that are as bold as you. Inspired by the Jester/Creator archetype, our brand celebrates play, color, and small delightful surprises that make hydration fun.

Our mission

To help people express themselves while choosing sustainable, well-designed products. We use recycled materials where possible and design for longevity — because jokes are fun, but waste isn't.

Values

  • Playful design
  • Accessibility & inclusivity
  • Sustainability
  • High-quality craftsmanship
